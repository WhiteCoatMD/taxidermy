Our Story

A Family
Built on
Tradition

Lonely Pines Taxidermy is a proudly family-run shop operated by a father-and-son team out of Freeport, Ohio. Every mount that walks through our door is treated with the same respect and craftsmanship we'd give our own trophies.

We specialize in deer, turkey, and waterfowl — the animals that matter most to Ohio hunters. From the moment you bring us your harvest, we work with precision and care to capture the life and character of your animal in a lasting memorial.

How It Works

Our
Process

From field to wall, we guide you through every step to ensure your mount exceeds expectations.

01

Bring In Your Trophy

Drop off your harvest at our shop in Freeport, Ohio. We'll assess the animal, discuss your vision for the mount, and give you an honest timeline and quote.

02

Choose Your Mount Style

Pick from shoulder mounts, pedestal mounts, full-body mounts, flying poses, fan displays, and more. We'll help you choose the pose that best showcases your animal.

03

Expert Preservation

Our father-and-son team applies skilled techniques — careful skinning, fleshing, tanning, and form fitting — to ensure the mount is built to last a lifetime.

04

Finishing Touches

Eyes set, fur brushed, feathers aligned, paint work blended — we obsess over every detail until the mount looks exactly as it did in the field.

05

Pick Up Your Memory

When your mount is ready, we'll reach out. Come see it in person and take home a piece of your hunt preserved for generations.
